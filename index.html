Benin City, Nigeria

Official Flag of Nigeria

Benin City, the historic capital of the ancient Benin Kingdom, is one of Nigeria’s oldest and most culturally vibrant cities. Famous for its bronze artwork and the Oba's Palace, it remains a living center of art, history, and tradition. Today, the city blends its rich heritage with modern life through bustling markets, festivals, and museums, making it a true heartbeat of Nigeria's cultural identity. It is one of the oldest cities in Nigeria and remains a center for art and history.
