Design Principles

Applying design principles for clean and accessible web layouts

Important Announcement

Always check your color contrast ratios! Visual accessibility is essential to ensure all users can read your content comfortably. Make sure your color combinations pass standard accessibility guidelines (AA or AAA contrast levels).

Design Concepts and Checklist

Good Design Balances

An effective design balances color, spacing, and typography. Grids bring structure; spacing brings rhythm. Consistency in fonts and alignment creates visual harmony.

Design Checklist

  • High color contrast
  • Clear visual hierarchy
  • Consistent spacing
  • Readable typography
  • Responsive layout
